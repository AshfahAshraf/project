Shipping Information

At Artisan Store, we aim to deliver your handcrafted products to you as quickly and safely as possible. Below are our shipping details

  1. Orders are processed within 1-2 business days.
  2. Delivery typically takes 3-7 business days depending on your location.
  3. You will receive a confirmation email once your order is shipped.

  1. Free shipping on orders above ₹500.
  2. A flat shipping charge of ₹40 applies to orders below ₹500.

  1. Once your order is shipped, we will send you a tracking number and tracking link via email or SMS.
  2. You can track your order status in real time.

  1. We currently ship across India.
  2. International shipping is not available at this time.

  1. Please contact us within 48 hours of delivery.
  2. Email us at: support@artisanstore.com
  3. Attach clear photos of the damaged item and packaging.
Our team will assist you as quickly as possible.
